Our Corporate Catering Services

1. Office Lunch Catering

  • Buffet Style:
    Jollof rice, fried rice, grilled chicken, beef, fish, plantains, salads
  • Plated Meals: Executive lunch boxes for individual servings
  • Small Chop Options: Mini puff-puff, spring rolls, samosas for light meetings

2. Breakfast Catering

  • Continental & Nigerian breakfast (akara & pap, yam & egg, sandwiches, fresh fruits)

  • Light snacks (finger foods, pastries, fruit platters)

4. Corporate Event & Gala Dinners

5. Special Dietary & Healthy Options

  • Vegan/vegetarian meals
  • Low-carb, diabetic-friendly, and gluten-free choices
