Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

+

We offer corporate catering, school meals, and private event catering featuring a diverse menu of Nigerian and international cuisines.

How can I place an order?

+

Orders can be placed via phone, email, or through our website order form. We recommend ordering at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you offer customized menus?

+

Yes, we provide customized menus tailored to meet your preferences, including dietary restrictions or specific requests.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours before the scheduled event for a full refund. Late cancellations may incur a fee.

What types of events do you cater?

+

We cater a variety of events including corporate meetings, seminars, school lunches, and private gatherings. We do not cater weddings or large parties.

Can I request a tasting session?

+

Yes, we offer tasting sessions for large corporate orders or events. Please contact us to schedule a session in advance.

Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?

+

Yes, we have a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Please let us know your preferences when placing your order.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

+

Yes, the minimum order quantity depends on the type of event. For corporate catering, a minimum of 10 servings is required. Please contact us for more details.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

+

Yes, we accommodate various dietary needs including gluten-free, nut-free, and lactose-free options. Please notify us of any allergies or restrictions when placing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept payments via bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. Payment details will be provided once the order is confirmed.

Do you provide delivery services?

+

Yes, we offer delivery services within our coverage area. Delivery fees may apply based on the location. We also offer pick-up options.
